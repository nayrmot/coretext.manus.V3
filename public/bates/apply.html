Bates Labeling Information
What is Bates Labeling?

Bates labeling is a method of indexing legal documents for easy identification and retrieval. Each page is assigned a unique identifier that typically includes a prefix, a sequential number, and sometimes a suffix.

Benefits
  • Unique identification of each document page
  • Easy reference during depositions and court proceedings
  • Simplified document organization and retrieval
  • Standardized document referencing across parties
Best Practices
  • Use consistent prefixes for each case
  • Maintain a registry of assigned Bates numbers
  • Apply labels to all pages of a document
  • Use sufficient padding for sequential numbers
