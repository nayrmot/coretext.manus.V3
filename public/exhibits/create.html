Exhibit Information
What are Exhibits?

Exhibits are documents or other items that are formally presented as evidence in a legal proceeding. They are typically marked with an exhibit number or letter for easy reference.

Exhibit Stickers

Exhibit stickers are labels applied to documents to identify them as exhibits. The system can automatically apply exhibit stickers to the first page of documents.

Exhibit Packages

Exhibit packages are collections of exhibits grouped together for a specific purpose, such as a deposition, hearing, or trial. Creating packages makes it easier to manage and distribute related exhibits.

Best Practices
  • Use consistent exhibit numbering within each case
  • Provide clear, descriptive names for exhibits
  • Organize exhibits into packages based on their use
  • Apply Bates labels before creating exhibits
