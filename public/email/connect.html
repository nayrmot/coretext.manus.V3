Email Integration Help
About Gmail Integration

The Document Management System can monitor your Gmail account for new emails and automatically process attachments as documents.

Features
  • Automatically import email attachments as documents
  • Monitor specific Gmail labels
  • Apply Bates labels to imported documents
  • Categorize documents based on email content
  • Receive notifications when new documents are processed
Privacy & Security

The system only accesses emails in the labels you specify. Your Gmail credentials are securely stored and can be revoked at any time.
